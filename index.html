About Me

I am a highly accomplished professional and co-trainer with the Food Science and Technology Association, where I play a pivotal role in supporting lead trainers and delivering comprehensive education on food safety, quality, and technology. In addition to my responsibilities as a co-trainer, I also serve as a distinguished food consultant, providing valuable insights to a diverse clientele and focusing on optimizing food service operations.

I am proud to be an esteemed alumna of the YSEALI Professional Fellow Program, where I successfully completed a fellowship in Sustainable Development and Environment at ProduceGood. This experience underscores my unwavering commitment to sustainable practices. My participation in the ENGAGE program further highlights my dedication to driving positive change, complemented by voluntary contributions to the Civic Society Initiative.

With a Master's in Food Technology, my research is dedicated to addressing challenges related to food wastage, showcasing my expertise in the field of food science and technology. I am passionate about making meaningful contributions to the industry and fostering positive transformations within the realm of food service and sustainability. .
